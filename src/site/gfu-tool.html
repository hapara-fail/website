Read "Using the Google Form Unlocker Safely" prior to using this tool.
Step 1 of 3

Open The Form

In a new browser tab, open the locked Google Form link. Stop at the "Locked mode is on" screen. Do not start the quiz.

Step 2 of 3

View Page Source

In the address bar of the form tab, type view-source: directly before the https://... and press Enter.

Step 3 of 3

Unlock The Form
